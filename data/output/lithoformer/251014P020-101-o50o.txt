<b>Chapter 7 Quiz:<br>Somatic Symptom and Dissociative Disorders</b><br><br>[Ariana, whose father died of a heart attack when Ariana was 11 years old, has long worried that she, too, will die suddenly when she notices an increase in her breathing and heart rate.
